# BreakLoop — Alternatives Starter Pack (v1)

**Source inspiration:** _Overcoming Phone Addiction Triggers_ (user-provided PDF) + behavior-design best practices.  
**Purpose:** Curated alternative activities for each internal trigger/root cause, ready to seed the “Alternatives” tab.  
**Implementation note:** No **duration** field (per your request). Users can **add / edit / delete / favorite** later.

---

## Suggested activity fields (for later implementation)

- **title**: short, action-oriented
- **reason**: one of {Boredom, Stress/Anxiety, Lonely, Fatigue, Self-doubt, No clear goal}
- **instructions**: 1–3 lines (what to do now)
- **effort**: Low / Medium / High
- **context_tags**: list (examples: `no_phone`, `outside`, `social`, `movement`, `calm`, `creative`, `planning`, `at_home`, `no_feed`)
- **phone_allowed**: Yes/No  
  - (Some are “intentional phone use”, e.g., calling a friend. Avoid feeds.)
- **setup**: None / Minimal / Needs item (optional)
- **notes**: optional extra hint
- **is_favorite**: boolean (default false)

> Each list is ordered **most effective → less effective** (direct need first, then lighter backups).

---

# 1) Boredom
Boredom is fuel for exploration and learning. These alternatives convert boredom into **curiosity + action**.

1) **Curiosity Question Trio**  
- instructions: Write 3 questions you’re curious about *right now*. Pick 1 to explore later (not via scrolling).  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `no_phone`, `creative`, `at_home`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

2) **Micro-Explore Outside**  
- instructions: Step outside and notice 5 specific details (colors, sounds, textures). Let your mind wander without input.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `outside`, `movement`, `no_phone`, `calm`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

3) **Learn One Small Thing (Intentional, No Feeds)**  
- instructions: Choose one topic and read *one* focused page/article (no infinite scroll). Stop when done.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `learning`, `at_home`, `no_feed`  
- phone_allowed: Yes (intentional)  
- setup: Minimal  
- notes: Use reader mode/bookmark; avoid feed platforms.

4) **Make One Tiny Improvement**  
- instructions: Improve one area: clear 10 items or wipe one surface.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `at_home`, `movement`, `practical`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

5) **Start a “2-Min Project Step”**  
- instructions: Pick a project (fitness, language, hobby). Do the smallest next step: set up materials, open notebook, prep.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `creative`, `growth`, `at_home`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

6) **Novelty Without Screens**  
- instructions: Change one variable (room, route, posture, silence). Let boredom reset attention naturally.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

7) **Read 2 Pages**  
- instructions: Read 2 pages of a book. Stop after 2 pages if you want.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `learning`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Needs item (book)

8) **Creative Doodle / Sketch**  
- instructions: Draw patterns/shapes for a moment. No goal—just expression.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `creative`, `calm`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Needs item (paper/pen)

# 2) Stress / Anxiety
Stress needs **downshifting** and **regaining control**—not more input.

1) **Physiological Sigh ×3**  
- instructions: Inhale → top-up inhale → long slow exhale. Repeat 3 times.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

2) **Box Breathing**  
- instructions: 4 in → hold 4 → 4 out → hold 4. Repeat a few cycles.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

3) **Grounding: 5–4–3–2–1**  
- instructions: Name 5 things you see, 4 feel, 3 hear, 2 smell, 1 taste.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

4) **Write the Worry → Next Action**  
- instructions: Write the worry in one sentence. Then write the next tiny action (even 1 minute).  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `planning`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

5) **Move to Discharge Stress**  
- instructions: 10 squats / quick stretch / short brisk walk.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `movement`, `outside`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

6) **Tidy One Surface**  
- instructions: Clear one small area (desk corner). Reduce visual noise; regain control.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `practical`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

7) **Cold Water Reset**  
- instructions: Splash cold water on face or hold something cool briefly.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

8) **Compassion Sentence**  
- instructions: “This is hard. I’m safe right now. One step at a time.”  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

- context_tags: `social`  
- phone_allowed: Yes (intentional)

- instructions: Propose a concrete time/place (even short).  
- effort: Medium

- phone_allowed: Yes (intentional)  
- setup: None

- setup: Minimal

5) **Small Act of Care**

- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `social`  
- phone_allowed: Optional  
- setup: Minimal

6) **Connection List**

8) **Join a Scheduled Group**

# 4) Fatigue
Fatigue is a body signal: you need **rest, fuel, or reduced stimulation**.

1) **Hydrate + Slow Breaths**  
- instructions: Drink water. Take 5 slow breaths to shift out of stimulation.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

2) **Eyes & Neck Reset**  
- instructions: Look far away for 20–30 seconds. Gently roll shoulders/neck.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `movement`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

3) **Power Rest (No Input)**  
- instructions: Lie down, close eyes, and rest quietly. No content.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

4) **Light Walk / Daylight**  
- instructions: Go outside briefly. Daylight + movement helps reset fatigue.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `outside`, `movement`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

5) **Eat Something Simple**  
- instructions: If you haven’t eaten, get a small balanced snack (protein + carbs).  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `practical`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

6) **Smallest Useful Thing**  
- instructions: Do one tiny task that reduces friction later (prep clothes, clear one item).  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `practical`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

7) **Reduce Input**  
- instructions: Lower lights, remove noise, sit quietly. Recovery needs less stimulation.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

8) **Early Night Setup**  
- instructions: Set up a better sleep environment (water by bed, dim lights, tidy bed).  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `practical`, `calm`, `at_home`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

# 5) Self-doubt

1) **Evidence List (3 Wins)**  
- instructions: Write 3 things you did well (even small). Evidence beats feelings.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`, `at_home`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

2) **One “Good Enough” Action**  
- instructions: Pick one task and do the smallest “good enough” step. Confidence follows action.  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `planning`, `practical`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: Minimal

3) **Reframe Sentence**  
- instructions: Replace “I can’t” with “I’m learning / not yet.” Then do one small step.  
- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No  
- setup: None

4) **Ask for One Clear Input**  
- instructions: Message someone you trust: “Can you give me one quick perspective?” (No feeds.)  
- effort: Medium  
- context_tags: `social`  
- phone_allowed: Yes (intentional)  
- setup: None

- effort: Low  
- context_tags: `calm`, `no_phone`  
- phone_allowed: No
